About

The AI Behind the Art

The AI Multiverse Museum is a generative art project exploring what happens when AI is asked to bridge two competing fictional universes — DC and Marvel — through character fusion.

Each piece was generated using ChatGPT / DALL·E, guided by carefully constructed prompts designed to balance both characters' visual identities, power sets, and aesthetic tones into a single coherent image.

The results reveal something interesting: AI doesn't just blend surfaces. Given detailed prompts, it finds the conceptual overlap between characters — the dark brooding shared by Batman and Iron Man, the chaos energy linking Harley Quinn and Scarlet Witch — and renders those shared qualities forward.
